Adrenalan Hosted by Red Bull & Bell Canada 2018


Adrenalan was a LAN Party + Hackathon event hosted by Red Bull and Bell Canada at the International Center in Brampton during the first week of August 2018. The theme of the hackathon revolved around gaming. We worked hard on the project, and fortunately there was time for us to check out the different attractions offered. Competitive tournaments where held on the big screen, consoles to battle friends, and endless refreshments provided by Red Bull. In the end, our team was proud to have snatched the 3rd place spot, and we learnt a lot from fellow groups competing and the workshops provided.

Our project aimed to reduce the amount of lag found in video games, something every gamer gripes about, using the power of machine learning to predict the movement of objects. The project was aptly named after that objective, "Flow.AI", to use AI to make games flow better. We used a test game, built on a simple webpage, and used Neataptic, a machine learning library for JS in the back end to train a neural network. The network was used to make predictions as to where the objects were moving.
