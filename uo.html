UOttahack at the University of Ottawa 2019


UOttahack was one of my only hackathons far away from my home city of Toronto, and the trip to the capital city was definetly a memorable one. Ottawa is a beautiful city, escpecially in the wintertime, allowing us to skate along the frozen Rideau Canal, and sightsee around the city. The students at the University of Ottawa did a stellar job organising the event and the weekend was a blast.

We decided to tackle problems surrounding automated driving. We thought that by using a blockchain netowrk, we could connect cars together to form a network.
